Privacy Policy

Bible Quiz Arcade (iOS)

Effective Date

Effective: December 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Bible Quiz Arcade (“the App”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

Summary

  • Gameplay data: The App stores progress (coins, XP, themes/unlocks, achievements) on your device and may sync it using Apple CloudKit.
  • Advertising: The App may show ads using Google AdMob (including rewarded ads and native ads). Ad providers may collect device/usage data.
  • Purchases: The App offers in-app purchases (e.g., “No Ads in Home”) through Apple’s App Store. The App does not store your payment details.
  • No account required: The App does not require you to create an account.

Information We Collect

1) Information you provide

The App does not require you to submit personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to use core features. If you contact us for support, you may provide information in your message (such as an email address).

2) Information collected automatically

Depending on configuration and usage, the App and third-party services may collect certain information automatically, such as:

  • Device information: device type, operating system version, language, region, and identifiers (as permitted by iOS settings).
  • Usage information: interactions with the App, ad impressions/clicks, and performance diagnostics.
  • Approximate location: derived from IP address by third-party ad services (if applicable).

3) Gameplay and progress data

The App stores gameplay progress locally on your device (for example: coins, XP, rounds completed, unlocked themes, and achievements). This data is used to provide gameplay features and restore progress.

CloudKit Sync (Apple iCloud)

If you are signed into iCloud on your device and iCloud is available, the App may sync certain gameplay/progress data using Apple CloudKit (private database under your iCloud account). This is used to help restore your progress across reinstalls/devices.

  • Data synced may include: coins, XP, themes/unlocks, achievements, and similar progress fields.
  • Who can access: This data is stored in your iCloud private database and is intended to be accessible only to you and Apple’s iCloud services.
  • Control: You can disable iCloud for the App in iOS Settings, which may limit cross-device restoration.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display ads (including rewarded ads and native ads). AdMob and its partners may collect and process data to provide and measure ads, prevent fraud, and improve services.

  • Data AdMob may collect: device identifiers (as permitted), IP address, approximate location, ad performance, and interaction data.
  • Personalized ads: Depending on your settings and region, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.
  • Opt-out controls: You can adjust ad tracking permissions via iOS settings (e.g., “Allow Apps to Request to Track”).

Learn more: Google Ads Technology and Google Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App may offer in-app purchases (such as removing ads on the Home screen). Purchases are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card information.

  • Purchase processing: handled by Apple.
  • Entitlements: the App may check purchase status using Apple’s StoreKit services to unlock features.

Apple’s privacy information: Apple Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core functionality: save and restore gameplay progress.
  • Improve the App: fix bugs and improve performance.
  • Advertising: display and measure ads and prevent ad fraud (via AdMob).
  • Purchases: validate and restore in-app purchase entitlements (via Apple StoreKit).

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information in these cases:

  • With service providers: such as Google AdMob (ads) and Apple CloudKit/StoreKit (sync & purchases).
  • For legal reasons: if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Data Retention

  • On-device: gameplay data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear data (if applicable).
  • CloudKit: synced gameplay data may remain in your iCloud private database unless you disable iCloud or delete relevant iCloud data.
  • Advertising data: handled by AdMob and subject to Google’s retention policies.

Your Choices

  • Ad tracking controls: manage tracking permissions in iOS Settings.
  • iCloud controls: enable/disable iCloud access for the App in iOS Settings.
  • Purchases: you can restore eligible purchases using the “Restore” button in the Store section (if available).

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction) without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

International Users

If you use the App from outside your country, your information may be processed in other countries through service providers (such as Apple and Google) consistent with their policies and applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective Date” above when changes are made.
